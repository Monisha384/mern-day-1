My Hobbies

Hobbies are activities that I enjoy doing in my free time. They help me relax, learn new things, and make my day more interesting. One of my favourite hobbies is reading. Books take me to different worlds, improve my vocabulary, and inspire my imagination. I also love listening to music because it gives me peace and refreshes my mind whenever I feel tired.Another hobby I enjoy is drawing. It allows me to express my creativity through colours and shapes . Sometimes, I draw nature scenes, animals, or characters from my imagination. I also enjoy spending time with my family and friends, playing outdoor games, and exploring new places.hese hobbies help me stay active, reduce stress, and keep a positive attitude in my daily life.
